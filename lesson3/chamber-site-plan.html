Target Audience

The target audience of the Houstonchamber.com web site is described below

  • Who: Various business entrepreneurs, aspiring company owners, and small business owners are looking to relocate to the Houston, Hoston Chambers favors tourists and individuals from all walks of life.

  • Age: The Houstonchamber.com website has no age limit. As long as you are able to surf the internet, this website is for you.

  • Motivation: Help various business owners to connect with each other, provide a brief information about the weather and the businesses in Houston Chamber of Commerce. Help residents navigate through different businesses in the area.
