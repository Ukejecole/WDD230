Galveston Island is a barrier island on the Texas Gulf Coast in the United States, about 50 miles southeast of Houston. The entire island, with the exception of Jamaica Beach is within the city limits of the City of Galveston in Galveston County

Market Square Park

Market Square Park is a public park in Downtown Houston, Texas, United States. It is bounded by Travis Milam, Congress and Preston Streets. It has reminded a geographic centerpiece of Downtown Houston since teh arrival of the City's founders, John Kirby and Augustus Chapman Allen in 1836

Nature Discovery Center

The Nature Discovery Centre is a plant and animal protection program that started as a joint initiative between the Houston Conservation Foundation and the now-defunct Houston Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperative. Nature Discovery Centre sists on 190 acres of land bordered by the Houston Lagoon. The program began as a way to protect Houston endangered species from going extinct

Redbud Art Center

Redbud Art Gallery is owned by Redbud foundations. The gallery is one of the largest of it's kind in Houston. Housed in five-storey Tall building in Westheimer, it boasts a collection of 8,000 diverse artworks from various local and international Nigerian artists like Chief Josephine Oboh Macleod
